About

Hello, my name is Carlos, also known as Azafran in the internet. I'm a graphic designer and illustrator (I mainly design t-shirts) and a 90s nostalgic.
You can find my work here. This site is just a fun project to practice with HTML and publish my Doom™ stuff in a retro manner (it reminds me of my frontpage days!). The page is carefully designed to weight as little as possible, so it loads fast and easy.
